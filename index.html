Message from the National President of the Alumni Association

Great Malabites and Malabresses! My name is Yakubu Ochefu and I was elected National President of our esteemed Alumni Association on the 27th of April, 2019. For those who may not know me, I graduated from Unical in 1982 from the Department of History. I then returned to my department in 1985 where I taught and subsequently received my doctorate degree in Economic History in 1992. I am currently a Professor of Economic History at the Benue State University, having served previously as a Deputy Vice-Chancellor at BSU, and a Vice-Chancellor at Kwararafa University, in Wukari, Taraba State. With the other eleven members of the National Alumni Executive Committee that were elected alongside me, we are working to implement programmes that will add value to our alma mater and members of the Alumni. The details of our plan of action which will be published shortly encompasses three major strategic pillars. Unity of purpose amongst all alumni; Engagement with our alma mater as a core value and Pursuit of the concept of an “Alumni Economy” that creates opportunities for members. I want to acknowledge to the role played by the Board of Trustees under the leadership of Rt. Hon. Bright Omokhodion, and the Electoral Committee and Members led by Barr, Paul Eshiemomoh in giving the alumni Association a new lease of life, a new constitution, and a new Executive Committee. I have chosen to take on the position of the President of the Alumni Association because with deep love I remember all that Unical gave me, and I am obliged by love to give back. Thus donating to and supporting our Alma Mater should be considered as an investment in its future. A quick glance over the schools current infrastructure will definitely inspire you in one way or another to help. I consider the presidency as an honor because I get to represent all of you. I look forward to it with zeal and enthusiasm.

Sincerely,

Prof. Yakubu A. Ochefu, Unical Alumni Association President.
